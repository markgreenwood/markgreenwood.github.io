About Mark

Mark is a former amateur bike racer and triathlete. Having been a runner most of his adult life, he started bike racing at 37, focusing on road races, and later began racing sprint-distance triathlons. Mark is an engineer who recently transitioned into full-time software development, and he created Velo Portal as a project to enhance his web development skills.

Mark lives in Portland, OR with his wife Michele, their twins Natalie and Tyler, and their dog Shadow.

About Velo Portal

Velo Portal is a demo site. The content (news, races, and marketplace items) are all "fake data" for the purpose of demonstrating the site concept. However, some of the news stories may contain factual content - who knows? If you raced at PIR in the early 2000s, it might seem oddly familiar.

In a fully-developed application, real news, race info, and marketplace data could be drawn from server databases or third-party APIs.

All photos on Velo Portal were taken by Mark or Michele Greenwood.
